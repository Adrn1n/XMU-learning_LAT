	-  Prefix: first $i (0 \le i \le n)$ characters; $n + 1$
	- Suffix: last $i (0 \le i \le n)$ characters; $n + 1$
	- Proper prefix: prefix excluding the original string; $n$
	- Substring: contiguous sequence of characters within the string (non-empty); $\frac{n (n + 1)}{2}$
	- Subsequence: sequence of characters extracted from the string, keeping the order; $2^n$
